Basic Electronics Components

Click a component to enlarge its image and learn more about it.

Resistor

Limits current in circuits.

Capacitor

Stores and releases electrical energy.

Inductor

Stores energy in a magnetic field.

LED

Emits light when current flows.

Diode

Allows current to flow in one direction.

Transistor

Amplifies or switches electrical signals.

Photodiode

Converts light into current.

Potentiometer

Variable resistor to adjust voltage.

Fuse

Protects circuit by breaking when overloaded.

Switch

Controls the flow of electricity.

Relay

Electrically operated switch.

Battery

Provides electrical energy.

Transformer

Changes AC voltage levels.

Motor

Converts electrical energy to mechanical.

Oscillator

Generates repeating signals.

Crystal

Provides precise frequency reference.

Microcontroller

Programmable control unit.

ADC

Converts analog signals to digital.

DAC

Converts digital signals to analog.

Voltmeter

Measures voltage.

Ammeter

Measures current.

Piezo

Converts electrical signals to sound.

Sensor

Detects physical parameters.

LED Matrix

Grid of LEDs to display patterns.

Buzzer

Produces sound when powered.

Phototransistor

Light-sensitive transistor.

FET

Field-effect transistor.

Op-Amp

Amplifies voltage difference.

Logic Gate

Performs digital logic operations.

Power Supply

Provides regulated voltage and current.
